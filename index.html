TALK

02.

BUILDING EMOTION

How Music and Architecture
Shape Human Experience

"Inui Architects" is a Tokyo-based architectural firm that designs a wide range of genres, including public buildings such as stations and schools, welfare facilities, housing, interiors, and public plazas. In recent years, the firm has recognized the importance of "commons" (shared assets) and the "act of commoning" in society and explores architectural forms that nurture such commons. We also explore the various possibilities of the commons, drawing on our experience in "Learning from Little Spaces" research.

For projects of a more public nature, we emphasize the importance of local research and hearings. We place importance on accurately grasping the social significance and enhancing projects while conducting research from various perspectives. For welfare-related projects in particular, we consider not only planning to improve care but also the impact of the project on community development.

ABOUT US

TOTO Architect Talk is an annual events including architect ltalk and design contest organized by TOTO Vietnam Co., Ltd. in collaboration with TOTO Gallery MA, media sponsored by the Vietnam Association of Architects. TOTO Architect Talk is not only an opportunity to exchange and learn from Japanese Architects but also a chance for young architects to showcase their talents.

TOTO Vietnam Co., Ltd. hope to foster community activities in Vietnam, especially the architectural community, aiming to bring many practical values. This is also the direction throughout the TOTO corporation.
